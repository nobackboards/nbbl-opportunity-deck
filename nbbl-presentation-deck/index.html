Category Philosophy

REMIXING
THE GAME

Removing the backboard and reducing the hoop to 16 inches (UHoop) changes the geometry of the sport.

NBBL doesn't replace traditional basketball; it complements it. This creates a faster, more challenging game that rewards:

  • Precision & Touch: High-arcing, clean swishes.
  • Decision Making: Instant passing and rapid shot setup.
  • Skill Transfer: Translates directly back to traditional hoops.
⚡

The Futsal of Basketball

Just as Futsal developed world-class soccer legends (Pele, Ronaldinho) by training them in tight spaces with a heavier ball, No Backboard Basketball serves as the ultimate developmental environment for basketball touch, speed, and precision.
