Category Philosophy

REMIXING
THE GAME

Removing the backboard and reducing the hoop to 16 inches (UHoop) changes the geometry of the sport.

NBBL doesn’t replace traditional basketball; it supplements it. This creates a faster, more challenging game that rewards shooting touch and cognitive focus.

  • ■ Precision & Touch: High-arcing, clean swishes with no room for error.
  • ■ Decision Velocity: Constant pace creates cognitive load that improves game IQ.
  • ■ Skill Transfer: Neuro-motor adaptation translates directly back to traditional hoops.
⚡

The Futsal of Basketball

Just as Futsal developed world-class soccer legends (Pele, Ronaldinho) by training them in tight spaces with a heavier ball, No Backboard Basketball serves as the ultimate developmental environment for basketball touch, speed, and precision.
